My Musical Journey and Recent Highlights
Music has been a lifelong passion of mine, starting at the age of 3 with piano lessons. After four years of formal training, I began self-learning, which expanded my musical horizons. By grade 7, I was delving into music production through YouTube tutorials, and by grade 9, I was creating my own songs. One of my breakthrough moments was an Anirudh mashup that showcased my musical skills and creativity, leading to more album projects.
During the COVID-19 pandemic, I honed my skills further by learning sound engineering after finishing school. This knowledge has been invaluable in my journey, allowing me to complete over 20 album songs. My career has also expanded into film work as a programmer, music director for short films, and as a lyricist and singer, with over 10 songs written and performed.
"One of my latest releases, "Barbie Bomma," features 90's school love memories with a peppy tune and dance moves set in a school campus, starring Jodi fame Sohail and Eruma Sani fame Aiswarya. The song has become a phenomenal hit on YouTube, resonating with many fans."
Additionally, I recently scored music for a 3-minute thriller short film published on Moviebuff for a contest. The response has been overwhelmingly positive, with viewers and industry professionals praising the score's ability to enhance the film's suspense and tension. Thank you for joining me on this musical journey. Stay tuned for more exciting projects and releases!
